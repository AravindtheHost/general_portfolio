In the long run, I aspire to establish myself as an AI or data scientist, contributing to the forefront of technological advancements and innovations. My portfolio showcases my dedication to these goals, featuring projects such as the "Car Rental System" and "Farmer Informant," which highlight my passion for technology's practical applications.
