About Me

Hey how is it going, I'm Aravind S, a 21-year-old computer science enthusiast from the picturesque town of Attukal in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. I was born on September 1, 2002, and my journey into the world of technology began with a deep-seated passion for computer science. My educational path has been marked by dedication and consistent achievement. During my secondary education at St. Joseph HSS, Trivandrum, I excelled in the field of computer science, earning a commendable score of over 75%. This solid foundation in computing propelled me to pursue higher studies, leading me to UIT Kaniramkulam, where I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA) with a remarkable CGPA of 77.06. Currently, I am embarking on a fascinating journey towards specialization in Artificial Intelligence (AI) at the prestigious Karyavattom Campus, Trivandrum, as part of my postgraduate program. My unwavering goal is to harness the power of AI and data science to create solutions that make a meaningful impact on society. In the short term, I'm committed to further enhancing my communication and soft skills, despite already having a solid foundation in these areas. Additionally, I stay dedicated to keeping my knowledge up-to-date in the ever-evolving IT field. In the long run, I aspire to establish myself as an AI or data scientist, contributing to the forefront of technological advancements and innovations. My portfolio showcases my dedication to these goals, featuring projects such as the "Car Rental System" and "Farmer Informant," which highlight my passion for technology's practical applications. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and please feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect or discuss potential collaborations.
